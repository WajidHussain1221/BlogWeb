Computer Science

Mon, May 25th 2020
O levels Computer Science is a subject that introduces students to fundamental concepts and skills in computer programming, software development, and information technology. It covers topics such as programming languages, data structures, algorithms, computer organization, and networking. Students will learn to design, implement, and debug computer programs using a range of programming languages and development tools. The course also emphasizes the importance of problem-solving and critical thinking in the context of computer science. Through practical assignments and projects, students will develop their analytical, programming, and communication skills, and be well-prepared for further studies in computer science or related fields.
